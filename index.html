Oruç Kahriman

Medical AI Researcher & Data Scientist

Berlin, Germany

Physicist turned data scientist, currently finishing a PhD (Dr. rer. med.) at Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin in biosignal data analysis. I work at ID Information und Dokumentation Berlin GmbH on the TRANSFER research project, building ML pipelines to predict intraoperative hypotension events in near-real time from operating room data. My work sits at the intersection of medical biosignal analysis, device regulation, and agentic AI.
